Size Guide

Measure like a real one. Get the perfect streetwear fit.

HOW TO MEASURE

All fellaż pieces are designed with a relaxed, true‑to‑size streetwear fit.

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline (above belly button).

Length

For tops: from the highest point of shoulder to desired hem.
