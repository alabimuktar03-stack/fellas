Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about fellaż.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also log into your account (if you created one) and view your order status. Tracking updates may take 24-48 hours to appear.

Can I cancel or change my order?

We process orders quickly. If you need to cancel or modify your order, contact us within 2 hours of placing it. After that, your order may have already been processed for shipping.

I received a wrong/damaged item. What do I do?

We're sorry! Contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item and your order number. We'll arrange a replacement or refund immediately.

How long does shipping take?

Nigeria: 3-7 business days
International: 10-20 business days depending on your location. Customs clearance may add extra time.

How much is shipping?

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. Free shipping on orders over ₦50,000 within Nigeria.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes! fellaż ships to most countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Customs fees may apply and are the customer's responsibility.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with all tags attached. We offer store credit or size exchange. Final sale items are not returnable.

How do I start a return?

Contact us via email at Fellaz07@gmail.com or WhatsApp with your order number. We'll provide return instructions within 24 hours.

Who pays for return shipping?

Customers cover return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

How do fellaż clothes fit?

Our tees and tops have a relaxed, true-to-size streetwear fit. Hoodies are slightly oversized. Check our Size Guide for detailed measurements.

What materials do you use?

We use premium cotton blends, heavyweight fleece for hoodies, and durable fabrics that last. All our products are designed for comfort and longevity.

How do I care for my fellaż pieces?

Machine wash cold inside out, tumble dry low, or hang dry. Do not bleach or dry clean. This keeps the graphics and fabric fresh longer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and mobile money. Secure payment processing via Paystack.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your full payment details. All transactions are processed through secure gateways.

Can I use multiple discount codes?

Only one discount code can be applied per order. Some items may be excluded from promotions.
