RETURNS & EXCHANGES

Returns Policy

Hassle-free returns. Because real ones deserve peace of mind.

14-Day Return Window

You have 14 days from delivery to request a return. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with all original tags attached.

Store Credit or Exchange

We offer store credit or size exchange on all eligible returns. Refunds to original payment method are available for defective items only.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error. We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

Processing Time

Once we receive your return, allow 5-7 business days for inspection and processing. You'll receive an email confirmation with your store credit or exchange details.

Final Sale Items

Items marked "Final Sale" or purchased during clearance events cannot be returned or exchanged. This includes mystery boxes and limited drops.
